Mandarin Cheesecake

I will write down a "Mandarine Cheesecake". This is my favorite cake and I can't stop eating it!
This is just a simple recipe from the website: Einfachbacken.de


Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 75g sugar
  • 75g soft butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1TL baking powder

For the topping

  • 500g lowfat quark
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pck vanille pudding
  • 1 pck vanille sugar
  • 200ml milk
  • 125ml oil
  • 120g sugar
  • 2 cans mandarins

Instructions

  1. For the shortcrust pastry, knead together the flour, sugar, butter, egg, and baking powder. Grease a springform pan (Ø26cm) and line it with the dough. Press the dough into place, forming a rim about 3 cm high.
  2. For the topping, mix together the low-fat quark, egg, pudding powder, vanilla sugar, milk, oil, and sugar. Place the mandarin oranges in a sieve and let them drain. Spread half of the mandarins over the shortcrust pastry base.
  3. Slowly pour the quark mixture over the top (so the mandarins stay in place). Preheat the oven to 180°C top/bottom heat (160°C convection). Carefully arrange the remaining mandarins in circles on top of the quark mixture. Bake the cake in the preheated oven for about 60 minutes. Let it cool completely. The cake yields about 12 pieces.
